    "Datasets can be filtered on the number of *dimensions*, *samples*, *timesteps*, *labels* and on *dataset name*.\n",
    "\n",
    "For example, the following are valid filter:\n",
    "\n",
    "* ``n_dims<=2``: filter datasets with fewer than 3 dimensions\n",
    "* ``dataset=~ProjectA``: filter datasets with *ProjectA* in its name\n",
    "* ``n_labels>2``: filter datasets with more than 2 labels\n",
    "\n",
    "\n",
    "Wildboar currently support the following subjects:\n",

    "* ``n_dims``\n",
    "* ``n_samples``\n",
    "* ``n_timestep``\n",
    "* ``n_labels``\n",
    "* ``n_dataset``\n",

    "\n",
    "* ``=~``: contains\n",
